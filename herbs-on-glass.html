Herbs on Glass

Glass Sculpture | Glass as a metaphor for environmental cultivation

Overview

I explored glass as a scultpural medium by creating an artificial environment for herbs to seed, take root, and grow from a piece of glass. The purpose was to express contrast between human interaction with the natural world and the cultivation of our environments. The herbs grew for 20 days.
