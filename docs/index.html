About Me

Hello!

I'm a UX designer dedicated to understanding the user’s story to create beautiful experiences.

I explore the user’s story by finding what they are looking for to help them meet their needs and achieve their goals. I work to make the user experience memorable and make it matter.

My strengths are in user research, storytelling, and human centered design. I also have a background in teaching, spending the last few years living and teaching in Vietnam, taking every chance I had to get on my motorcycle and ride across the entire country, to photograph and write about amazing places I come across and the extraordinary people I meet.

I’ve discovered that powerful places and powerful experiences with someone else can change your state of mind. Even if you cannot speak their language, you can still laugh with them and share in how they live, what they see, how they feel, eat the food they eat.

Stories have always evoked curiosity and wonder for me. I've realized that storytelling is at the core of what it means for me to be human. We can learn how to feel what others feel and enlighten each other. With it I hope to inspire creation, and to bring compassion and happiness into people’s lives. That’s beautiful. That's magic.

Enjoy life. Care about what you do.
