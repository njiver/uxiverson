Wine Bottles

Glass Sculpture | Stretching glass at 1300 degrees

Overview

I collected dozens of diffently colored bottles to stretch inside of a 24" x 34" electric kiln. I fabricated a steel armature to stretch these at 1300 degrees farenheit inside of the kiln. A slowed firing resulted in divitrification of the bottles. The newly anealed forms resemble anemones and aquatic plant life.
