Wood and Metal Lamp

Furniture Design | Repurposing scrap materials for design

Overview

I created this lamp from wood and metal scrap. The shade hangs freely over the lamp as it is supported by a continuous helix of steel. The wood shade creates a warm diffused light when the lamp is turned on and the steel reacts like a spring when it is touched.
